I am final year Master's student in Artificial Intelligence at the Polytechnic University of Catalonia, having completed dual bachelor's degrees in Computer Science and Statistics at the same university. My research interests spans Theoretical Machine Learning, Autoregressive Models, Optimization Techniques, and in general first-principles modeling.

I was awarded the Balsells mobility fellowship to study at the University of California, Irvine, where I completed my Master's Thesis under the supervision of Prof. Padhraic Smyth. My thesis focused on wildfire prediction, employing autoregressive and generative models to forecast wildfire spread patterns.
